An Initiative Project - TCG Design for Game DOTA2

I made this game because I love DOTA2. But I realised that I don't have enough time to play it. It is clear that many players have finally retired from this game, mostly the main reason is because of their age or the amount of time they spend with their family. I created a concept that will make you feel nostalgic about playing this game. It uses familiar characters and mechanics. It has simple, easy-to-access gameplay. For this project, I borrowed most of the assets from the original game and created a card concept based on the original lore.
